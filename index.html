Recipe Title

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes. I ame xcited to share, i have done this recipe with shrimp, and added vegetables such as peas and spinach. Usually this meal lasts me for several days as I cook 2 chicken breasts at a time. I will add more to this later.

Ingredients

    alfredo sauce
    pasta
    complete seasoning
    garlic
    butter
    chicken
    red pepper flakes
    salt
    water

Steps

    Bring water to a boil and add salt.
    In another pan, add marinated chicken or season prior to placing in pan.
    Cook chicken on one side until no pink on underside.
    Add pasta to boiling water.
    Turn chick on other side.
    Remove cooked chicken from pan and slice.
    Add alfredo sauce and stir.
    Add minced garlic, complete seasoning, and red pepper flakes to alfredo sauce and stir.
    Add sliced chicken to sauce.
    Turn heat from pasta.
    Add pasta to sauce and mix.
