Recipe Title

Description

I asked this cute girl a while back what she had for breakfast because I needed food ideas, and she said a salmon croissant sandwich. She wrote down the steps to create this magnificent meal from the almighty air fryer. I've yet to replicate the meal so this so I never lose it (and her).

Ingredients

    salmon/ul>
      blackened seasoning
      complete seasoning
      croissant
      butter

    Steps

      Fire up the air fryer.
      Butter croissants and place on stove.
      Season salmon and place in air fryer on parchment paper.
      Create sandwich once salmon is done.
